Teaching

My teaching centers on helping students understand American political institutions through evidence-based reasoning. I emphasize the connection between scholarly research and contemporary political questions, encouraging students to think critically about how political actors — legislators, donors, and voters — navigate uncertainty and pursue their interests within institutional constraints.
